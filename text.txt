Welcome to Acme Robotics.

Office Hours
------------
The office is open Monday through Friday from 08:00 to 18:00.

Vacation Policy
---------------
Employees receive 25 days of paid annual leave.
Vacation requests must be submitted at least two weeks in advance.

Remote Work
-----------
Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval.

IT Support
----------
For password resets, email helpdesk@acmerobotics.example.

Expense Policy
--------------
Meals are reimbursed up to €35 per day while traveling.
Hotel expenses require manager approval if they exceed €180 per night.

Meeting Rooms
-------------
Meeting rooms can be reserved using the internal booking portal.
